Customer Support Knowledge Base

========================
Refund Policy
=============

Customers may request a refund within 14 days of receiving their order.

Conditions for refunds:

* The product must be unused and in its original packaging.
* Proof of purchase or order ID is required.
* Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after approval.

Non-refundable items:

* Digital products
* Gift cards
* Products marked as final sale

If the item arrives damaged or defective, customers are eligible for a full refund or replacement.

========================
Shipping Policy
===============

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation.

Estimated delivery times:

* Domestic shipping: 3–5 business days
* International shipping: 7–14 business days

Customers will receive a tracking number via email once the order has been shipped.

Shipping delays may occur due to:

* Weather conditions
* Customs processing
* High seasonal demand

If a package is lost during shipping, customers should contact support within 7 days.

========================
Cancellation Policy
===================

Customers may cancel their order within 24 hours of placing it.

Cancellation rules:

* If the order has not been shipped, the cancellation will be processed immediately.
* If the order has already been shipped, cancellation is no longer possible.

In such cases, customers may request a return after receiving the product according to the refund policy.

========================
Replacement Policy
==================

If a product arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, customers may request a replacement.

Requirements:

* The request must be submitted within 7 days of delivery.
* Photos of the damaged or incorrect product may be required.

Replacement items will be shipped at no additional cost once the request is approved.

========================
Customer Support Guidelines
===========================

When responding to customers:

* Always be polite and professional.
* Acknowledge the customer's issue.
* Provide clear instructions on the next steps.
* Reference the relevant company policy when necessary.
* Offer solutions such as refunds, replacements, or support guidance when applicable.
